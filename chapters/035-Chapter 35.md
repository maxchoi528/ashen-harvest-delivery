Not the hurried tread of Dawn soldiers. Slow. Deliberate. Measured.
Kael turned. Vane raised her blade.
A figure stepped through the smoke.
Tall. Gaunt. Robes the color of dried blood. A mask covering the face—white porcelain, featureless except for carved eyes that reflected no light.
The Shadow Court.
"Commander Vane," the figure said. Its voice was soft, almost gentle. "The Council of Six sends their regards. And their demand."
Vane's blade trembled. "Explain."
