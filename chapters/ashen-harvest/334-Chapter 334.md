**[Soul Harvest: You extracted Memory Fragment (Corrupted) from the remains. Essence extraction failed—source corrupted.]**

A presence. Vast and cold and *aware*.

**[Soul Harvest: You extracted Spiritual Corruption (Stage 3) from the remains. Essence extraction failed—target not deceased.]**
