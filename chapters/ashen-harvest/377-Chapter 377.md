**[Soul Reaver: Harvest successful. You extracted 34 units of spiritual essence and the skill "Barrier Lance" from the remains. Quality: Rare.]**

**[Soul Reaver: Harvest successful. You extracted 28 units of spiritual essence and the ability "Iron Stance" from the remains. Quality: Uncommon.]**
