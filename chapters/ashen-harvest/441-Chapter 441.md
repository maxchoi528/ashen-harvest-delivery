Kael turned. The battle was ending. Cultists fled or died. The Shadow Court's agents secured the perimeter. The Rift itself had stabilized—not sealed, but no longer growing.
And on the ridge, watching everything, stood a figure in golden robes. The Order of Dawn's advance force, finally arriving.
One of them raised a hand—not in greeting, but in recognition. As if seeing something unexpected. As if realizing their centuries-long plan had just encountered an obstacle.
Kael met that gaze across the battlefield, and for the first time since his execution, felt something other than fear.
Curiosity.
The game had changed. The enemy had revealed itself. And Kael Ashborn—Divine-stage Soul Reaver, death-row survivor, monster of the new age—finally had a target worth hunting.
The Order of Dawn's representative lowered his hand and turned away.
