Kael pressed himself against a pillar, gasping for breath. His body trembled. The harvest had cost him—not spiritually, but mentally. He could still feel the Entity's presence at the edge of his consciousness, waiting.
The woman in white turned to him. "Ashborn. You're coming with us."
"I didn't—"
"You touched it. The Entity. I saw you." Her eyes were accusation and calculation. "You're either the most valuable asset we've found in decades, or the most dangerous threat we've ever faced. The Conclave will decide which."
Guards surrounded him. Kael didn't resist.
He had found Zarek. He had learned the truth about the seals. He had even harvested something from an Entity itself—though he couldn't access it yet.
But the Order had him now. And somewhere in the shadows, the Whisper King was watching.
The woman gestured, and the Enforcers dragged Kael toward the stairs.
