They would know about him.

"Draw them out," he said. "Make noise. Draw two of them to the stairwell. I'll take the other two through the service shaft."

Vance hesitated. Then she pulled a flare from her belt and lit it, the red glow casting harsh shadows across the stone walls.
"Two minutes," she said. "Then I'm coming down whether you're in position or not."
She ran. Kael followed a different path.

They were talking about him. They had been planning this all along—the raid on the facility, the attack on the archive, the theft of the soul-fragments. All of it was a cover, a distraction. Their real objective was to capture him.
Or kill him and harvest whatever pieces they could extract from his corpse.
"Impressive infiltration."

"Ser Vance didn't know about me," the cultist said. "None of them did. The Mortician's Guild believed they had sealed all the rifts in this facility. They were wrong."

Kael moved.

It didn't work.

**[Soul Harvest: You extracted Entity Communion (partial) from the remains. Memory fragment acquired.]**

The Whisper King.

The Soul Reaver's origin. The Whisper King's identity. The seventy-two sealed rifts and the Entities they imprisoned.
