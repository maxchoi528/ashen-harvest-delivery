Two hundred years, according to the shadow markets. Two centuries of patient preparation.

Ordinary citizens going about their evening, unaware that a man had died thirty yards from their homes.

Two hundred years. Kael filed the confirmation away. The Whisper King had been planning for two centuries, and recent events had accelerated the schedule. If it could be removed.

Three harvests until the entity could walk through him into the Material realm.

Outside Thornwick, fog covered the city like a burial shroud. And in that fog, schemes intertwined—Whisper King's two-century plan, Faceless One's fragment-growing timeline, Shadow Court's mysterious interest, Order Council's impending response.
