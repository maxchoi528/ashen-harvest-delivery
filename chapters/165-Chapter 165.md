[Soul Harvest: You extracted residual soul-essence from the remains. 12 points acquired.]

First Soul Reaver. Name damaged. Title intact.

[Soul Harvest: You extracted degraded memories from the remains. Fragments acquired.]

Marcus Thorne. The cult's eastern coordinator. He'd been operating out of a warehouse in the Ashwick district for the past six months, coordinating cell activities and reporting to someone called only the Architect.
