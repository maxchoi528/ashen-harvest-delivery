[System Notification] Ambient spiritual disturbance detected. Three entities in range. Threat assessment: Low.

A pattern for summoning.

Somewhere in that maze of iron and steam, Inquisitor Vane hunted him. Somewhere else, the Shadow Court watched with their own agenda. Corpses. Industrial waste. Possibly cultist sentries. Kael could work with corpses. The rest was manageable.

Someone had organized this hunt, and organized hunters didn't give up.

Industrial accidents, mass casualties, spiritual residue that had never fully dissipated.

Inquisitor Vane had beat him here.

Not the cult attacking the facility. The cult *supplying* the facility.
Someone inside was working with the Abyssal forces, using sacrifices to fuel whatever research they'd been conducting. And that someone had connections to the same conspiracy that had framed him, killed Gwen's squad, and now positioned Inquisitor Vane as guardian of their operation.

Inquisitor Aldric Thorne, once celebrated for cracking a trafficking ring that had spanned three cities. The Entities. Abyssal powers, offering alliance to those willing to betray everything the Orders had built. Kael understood now why Gwen's squad had been sacrificed—collateral damage in a larger operation to isolate him, to drive him toward this moment where they could spring their trap.

Sera's sister. They were planning to use her in whatever ritual they'd prepared. The same woman Sera had spent six months searching for, about to become fuel for Abyssal summoning.

Thorne was working with the Shadow Court and the cult simultaneously, playing multiple factions against each other while pursuing his own agenda. Vane was a pawn, genuinely hunting Kael while serving conspirators who manipulated his orders. Unless the context was already inside him.

The memories that didn't belong to him, that had been there since before his Awakening, that he hadn't known how to access until now.

Thorne. The conspiracy. They weren't trying to free the Entities—they were trying to *become* them. Using forced Awakening, soul harvesting, and ritual magic to accelerate their own progression beyond natural limits.

Evidence. Thorne had been speaking openly about his conspiracy, unaware of Kael's presence. If he could record or transmit what he'd heard, Vane would have no choice but to investigate.

A recording that anyone with spiritual sensitivity could replay. Evidence that couldn't be easily dismissed.
