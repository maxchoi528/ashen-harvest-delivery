Kael's hand found his side. Warmth seeped between his fingers. His heart pumped double-time, not from pain but from the sudden clarity that came with genuine danger.
Three against one. Stage 3 Awakened, entity-touched, coordinated. The alley offered no escape. Behind him, the brick wall. Before him, three murders dressed in human skin.
His Soul Reaver whispered possibilities. Those bodies, once stopped, contained power. Abilities. Spiritual residue that could sharpen his senses, boost his strength, maybe even give him an edge against whatever nightmare wore the Faceless One's blessing.

He could kill these three. Take what they had. Walk out of this alley stronger than when he'd entered.

Three cultists. One wounded but mobile. One bleeding but alive. One shifting, morphing, clearly the leader.
The wounded one first. Easier. Faster. The law of diminishing returns suggested that the longer a fight stretched, the more chaotic it became. Chaos favored numbers. Chaos favored enemies.
