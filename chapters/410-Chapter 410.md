Beyond the shattered glass, the sky blazed with colors that shouldn't exist—auroras of sickly green and arterial red, pulsing with rhythms that matched Kael's racing heartbeat. A vial, filled with something that moved like liquid shadow. Soul-essence. But not the muddy brown of common harvests—this was pure, concentrated, black as the void between stars. "Accept this. Complete your training. When the time comes, you will know what to do."
Kael stared at the vial, at the man offering it. Everything in his nature screamed that this was wrong, that trust was a luxury he couldn't afford, that Zarek's motives were as opaque as the Abyssal energy saturating the temple. Bigger than the Orders. Bigger than anything you've imagined."

The essence of a being that had touched the Divine and been thrown back into the Abyss, compressed into a form that a mortal body could contain.
